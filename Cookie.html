Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Algoriteam uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our apps.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our apps to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Authentication and security
  • Maintaining your session
  • Loading and saving your data

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our apps. This helps us:

  • Improve app performance
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most popular

2.3 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences, such as:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme settings (dark mode/light mode)
  • Display preferences

3. Cookie Details

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session
user_preferences Stores your app preferences 1 year
analytics_id Tracks app usage for improvements 2 years

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies, including:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Payment processors
  • Customer support tools

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

5.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Clear all cookies when you close the browser

5.2 App Settings

Within our apps, you can manage certain preferences in the Settings menu.

5.3 Opt-Out Links

For analytics cookies, you can opt out through:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out

6. Mobile App Tracking

Our mobile apps may use device identifiers and similar technologies for analytics and personalization. You can control this through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
